UAS FILES

It features vibrant, nostalgic artwork that really brings a cool, vintage vibe.
Plus, it cleverly incorporates references from the Netflix treasure hunt show 
with Justin Posey, as well as classic tales like Journey to the Center of the Earth. 
The theme also includes a fascinating clock section that nods to different historical eras, 
and even a fun sci-fi twist with an alien being interrogated using a truth serum.
Altogether, it's a really imaginative and immersive theme that brings a lot of creativity 
and cool references together.
